About

7 Women 6 Shorts was AbeoJacksonProductions' first-ever film screening event was held at the Digicel IMAX Theatre in 2019. It featured quirky, avant garde storytellers with powerful points of view.

Designed to encourage and create visibility for new work from budding storytellers and art activists, the screening featured 6 short films of varying styles and genres by women film makers of color with connections across the diaspora.

Featured Films

  • Nadia Huggins: Circa no future
  • Abeo Jackson: Ruth
  • Anne Musisi (Uganda/London): Steups
  • Shari Petti: Sorf Hair
  • Sonja Dumas: Tickle Me Rich
  • Taromi Lourdes Joseph: Hush-A-Bye
  • Fiona Compton (St Lucia/London): Silence
