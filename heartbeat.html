Heartbeat of a Nation

About

Heartbeat of a Nation is a heartrending, earth-shattering Rapso warrior monologue performed with fiery passion on behalf of the creative community of Trinidad and Tobago.

  • Mixing and Mastering: Rishi Mahato at Maha
  • Recording Studio: Maha Productions & OJO
  • Post Production: Marcus “Xbass” Maximin
