I am_Merikin

About

I am_Merikin charts the journey, history and stories handed down from one generation to the other, highlighting various contributions of the "Merikin Maroons" of Trinidad and how these narratives frame the current reawakening of black consciousness/advocacy and diasporic unity.

Created with funding from the Catapult Arts Carib Grant, I am_Merikin is a rallying call to other creatives to collaborate, to tell an important story. A story that is so intrinsically linked to taking control of our narratives as Caribbean, African descended people of the Diaspora. This collaborative effort could in no way have been achieved without the immeasurable contributions of time talent, energy and discipline, as well as a team's trust in a creative vision.

I am_Merikin was screened during the 2021 Film and Folklore Festival.

Genre: Documentary

Runtime: 50 min 56 sec
