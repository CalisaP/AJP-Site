About

Laugh and Cry Duz Live in de Same House was performed during the 2019 edition of Carifesta at the Trinidad Theater Workshop with support from the Ministry of Community Development, Culture and the Arts.

It featured an evening of intimate, personal, heartbreaking and scandalous work from the final group of young actors/mentees Raymond Choo Kong worked with during the 'Mentoring By The Masters' program.
