About

The moving monologue of a mother coming to grips with the tragic result of her daughter's mental health struggles.

Per AJP Executive and Creative Producer, Abeo Jackson:

So I've been reading lots of scenes, two handers and monologues online. Searching for what? I'm not sure yet. This piece made me nostalgic for an unfinished short film I had to abandon 2 years ago for lots of reasons that was shot in London that dealt with this kind of subject matter. And yes light and funny is always lovely, maybe even especially needed in times like these, but as a one of my acting coaches said to me once: Abeo you have an affinity for going to dark places. Apparently I'm comfortable there. So I'll go back to light and funny another day.

Genre: Drama

Runtime: 4 min 8 sec
