About

Hosted by Abeo Jackson and Amanda T. McIntyre, #rockDboat868 is a real, raw and riveting podcast covering all de tings that equal bittersweet TnT. From current events to cultural touchstones, these two black girls engage in the kind of anti-establishment Jamette discourse Trinidad and Tobago (and the wider diaspora) needs to hear.
