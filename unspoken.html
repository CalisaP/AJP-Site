About

Created for the 2020 COCO Dance Festival, which featured the work of 24 choreographers from 9 countries across the world, UN-SPOKEN speaks to the experience of black womanhood in a hostile world.

Per AJP Executive and Creative Producer, Abeo Jackson:

Women Women of color BLACK Women..... .......grapple with a strange dissonance sometimes. Manifest in our own skin, as we navigate the onslaught of overt and microaggressive: physical, emotional, mental and gender based violence, faced on a daily basis. The social and political constructs, ie. unreconciled inherited colonial legacies, forcibly attached to our skin, our complexion, our hair, our bodies, our voices, our sexuality, all while we battle to take control/ownership of our own narratives, are burdens almost too much to bear. Almost. Because even while we remain amongst the most unprotected, most violated and most appropriated, we still manage to thrive. We still manage to be extraordinary. Magic not of this realm. Alien.
